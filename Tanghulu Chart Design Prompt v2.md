Tanghulu Chart（糖胡芦图） Design Prompt v2.0

Project Goal | 项目目标

Design and implement a brand-new financial chart called:

设计并实现一种全新的金融图表：

Tanghulu Chart（糖胡芦图）

The goal is to create a new visualization language that integrates price information, volume distribution, and order flow imbalance (Delta) into a single chart element.

目标是在单个图形元素中同时表达价格信息、成交量分布信息以及买卖主动性（订单流失衡），探索一种新的金融可视化语言。

Core Problem | 核心问题

Traditional candlestick charts only describe:

传统K线图只能表达：

Open

High

Low

Close

However, they cannot show:

但无法表达：

Where trading activity actually occurred

How volume was distributed across prices

The dominant transaction price

The primary value area

Who initiated the trades (Aggressive Buyers vs. Aggressive Sellers)

市场真正在哪里成交

成交量在价格区间中的分布

主要成交价格

主要价值区域

买卖双方的主动性（究竟是多头在主动买入，还是空头在主动砸盘）

Two candles may have identical OHLC values while representing completely different market structures and internal buying/selling pressure.

两根OHLC完全相同的K线，可能对应完全不同的市场成交结构和内部多空压力。

Fundamental Concept | 基本理念

Price alone is incomplete. Volume alone is ambiguous.

仅有价格是不完整的。仅有总成交量是模糊的。

A chart should show:

图表不仅要表达：

where price traveled (价格到过哪里)

but also:

还要表达：

where trading activity occurred (市场真正在哪里成交)

who was in control at that specific price (在该具体价位谁掌握了控制权)

The Tanghulu Chart attempts to encode volume distribution and order flow direction directly into the chart itself.

糖胡芦图尝试将成交量分布与订单流方向直接编码进图形本身。

Important Constraint | 重要约束

The Tanghulu Chart is a 2D chart.

糖胡芦图是一个二维图表。

It is NOT:

它不是：

3D chart

3D surface

Mountain chart

Volume landscape

Volume terrain

三维图表

三维曲面

三维山峰图

地形图

体渲染图

The chart must remain within a standard financial chart coordinate system:

必须保持标准金融图表坐标体系：

X-axis = Time

Y-axis = Price

成交量分布与买卖失衡必须编码在图形内部，而不是引入第三个维度。

Volume distribution and buy/sell imbalance must be encoded inside the chart element rather than using a third dimension.

Data Model | 数据模型

Each trading session contains:

每个交易日包含：

Date

Open

High

Low

Close

Total Volume

In addition, volume must be distributed across multiple price levels, split by aggressor:

同时需要构建价格区间内多个价格层对应的成交量分布，并按主动成交方拆分：

Ask Volume (Buy Vol): Volume generated by aggressive buyers hitting the ask. (主买成交量)

Bid Volume (Sell Vol): Volume generated by aggressive sellers hitting the bid. (主卖成交量)

The implementation may use skewed multi-modal Gaussian distributions with random Delta ratios for simulation. The architecture should allow future replacement with real tick-by-tick or order flow data.

实现阶段可以使用带有随机Delta偏置的多峰高斯分布进行模拟。架构上应支持未来接入真实逐笔成交数据或订单流（Order Flow）数据。

Volume Distribution & Imbalance | 成交量分布与买卖主动性

The most important innovation.

这是整个设计最重要的部分。

Different price levels should carry different visual weights according to traded volume. Furthermore, the chart must visually separate aggressive buying from aggressive selling.

不同价格层应根据成交量拥有不同视觉权重。此外，图形必须在视觉上区分主买与主卖力量。

Requirement:

Split the visual representation symmetrically/asymmetrically. For example, the left side represents Ask Volume (Aggressive Buying), and the right side represents Bid Volume (Aggressive Selling).

If buyers dominate a specific price level, that side of the shape should bulge outwards visibly compared to the sellers' side.

Differentiate the two sides using distinct colors (e.g., Cyan for Buying, Magenta for Selling).

要求：

采用左右拆分或不对称的视觉表达。例如：左半边代表主买成交量，右半边代表主卖成交量。

如果在某个价格上买方极度激进，图形的该侧应明显向外鼓起（形成不对称的“糖胡芦”）。

使用高对比度的颜色区分买卖力量（如青色代表主买，洋红色代表主卖）。

POC

Point Of Control

Definition:

The price level with the highest total traded volume (Buy Vol + Sell Vol).

定义：

总成交量（主买+主卖）最大的价格。

The chart must make the POC easy to locate.

图形必须让用户能够快速识别POC位置。

Value Area

Definition:

The price range containing approximately 70% of total volume.

定义：

累计约70%成交量所在价格区间。

The chart must make this area identifiable.

图形必须能够体现这一价格区域。

展示约30-40个交易日。

Arrange them chronologically from left to right.

按时间顺序从左到右排列。

The chart should allow users to observe:

用户应能够观察：

趋势变化
