1 图表设计说明 Chart Design Notes

核心隐喻：一根竹签串着若干山楂——这正是中国街头小吃"糖葫芦"的形态。把它映射到金融数据上：

Core metaphor: a bamboo skewer threading several hawthorn berries — exactly the shape of the Chinese street snack "tanghulu". Map it onto market data:

  • 竹签（垂直线）= 当日 Low → High 的价格区间
    Skewer (vertical line) = the day's Low → High price range
  • 山楂（圆）= 每个价格层的成交量。圆的面积与该层成交量成正比，越胖的山楂代表该价格成交越密集
    Berry (circle) = volume at each price level. The circle's area is proportional to that level's volume — a fatter berry means heavier trading at that price.
  • 整串的"形状"即当日成交量在价格上的分布轮廓（Volume Profile）
    The whole string's silhouette is the day's Volume Profile — the distribution of volume across prices.

这样一根糖葫芦同时编码了：价格区间（竹签长度）、OHLC（开/收盘横线 + 竹签端点）、成交量分布（山楂大小）、成交重心（山楂密集区）、POC 与 Value Area。时间维度上多根糖葫芦横向排列，即可观察趋势与成交重心的迁移。

A single tanghulu thus encodes: price range (skewer length), OHLC (open/close ticks + skewer ends), volume distribution (berry sizes), trading center-of-gravity (berry-dense zone), POC and Value Area. Lining up many tanghulus along the time axis reveals trend and the migration of the trading center of gravity.

2 如何解读糖胡芦图 How to Read a Tanghulu Chart

  • 看竹签长短：当日振幅（High−Low）。长竹签 = 大波动日
    Skewer length = day's range (High−Low). Long skewer = volatile day.
  • 看山楂分布：山楂集中在哪段价格，就是当日的成交重心。山楂越聚拢，市场共识越强
    Berry distribution: where berries cluster is the day's trading center of gravity. Tighter cluster = stronger consensus.
  • 看最大山楂（POC）：金色描边环的那颗，是当日成交量最大的价格，即市场最认可的公允价格
    Largest berry (POC): the one with the gold ring — the price with the most volume, i.e. the market's fairest price.
  • 看色带（Value Area）：覆盖约 70% 成交量的价格区间，是当日价值区域。价格在 VA 内 = 合理区；突破 VA = 趋势确立
    Value Area band: the ~70%-volume price zone — the day's value area. Price inside VA = fair zone; breaking VA = trend confirmed.
  • 看开/收盘位置：左侧白线=开盘，右侧白线=收盘。收盘远离 POC 说明尾盘偏离当日共识
    Open/Close position: left tick = open, right tick = close. Close far from POC = close diverged from the day's consensus.
  • 看颜色：红串=上涨日，绿串=下跌日
    Color: red string = up day, green string = down day.
  • 跨日看迁移：POC 逐日抬升=重心上移（多头）；POC 下移=重心下移（空头）；VA 收窄=蓄势待变
    Cross-day migration: POC rising = gravity up (bullish); POC falling = gravity down (bearish); VA narrowing = coiling for a move.

3 相比传统 K 线的优势 Advantages over Candlesticks

  • 信息密度 ↑：K 线只有 4 个价格点；糖葫芦还多了整条成交量分布曲线、POC、VA
    Higher density: candlesticks have 4 price points; tanghulu adds the full volume-distribution curve, POC and VA.
  • 暴露"成本结构"：K 线看不出"当天主要在哪个价位成交"；糖葫芦一眼可见成交重心
    Reveals cost structure: candles can't show where most trading happened; tanghulu shows the center of gravity at a glance.
  • 识别假突破：价格突破但 POC/VA 没跟上 = 量价背离，突破有效性存疑
    Spot fake breakouts: price breaks but POC/VA don't follow = volume/price divergence; breakout suspect.
  • 判断吸筹/派发：山楂在某价位反复堆积而价格不动 = 吸筹；高位山楂扩散 = 派发
    Detect accumulation/distribution: berries piling at a price while price stalls = accumulation; berries spreading at highs = distribution.
  • POC 作为磁吸位：未来价格倾向于回到 POC 附近，是天然的支撑/阻力
    POC as magnet: price tends to return to POC — a natural support/resistance.
  • 趋势健康度：健康趋势中 VA 应沿趋势方向迁移，若 VA 横向堆积则趋势乏力
    Trend health: in a healthy trend VA migrates along it; sideways VA piling = trend weakening.

4 未来可扩展方向 Future Extensions

  • 接入真实逐笔数据：loadTicks() 已预留接口，传入真实 tick 聚合的 price-level volume 即可
    Real tick data: loadTicks() is reserved — feed in real tick-aggregated price-level volume.
  • 复合 Volume Profile：把多日山楂叠加成一条"侧视轮廓"，类似 Market Profile 的 TPO
    Composite profile: stack multi-day berries into a side silhouette, like Market Profile TPO.
  • VA 迁移轨迹：用曲线连接每日 POC，可视化"公允价格漂移"
    VA migration trail: connect daily POCs with a curve to visualize fair-price drift.
  • 异常山楂检测：自动标出远超均值的"大单价格层"（冰山单）
    Anomaly detection: auto-flag price levels far above the mean (iceberg orders).
  • 多周期叠加：周线糖葫芦 + 日线糖葫芦的双层结构
    Multi-timeframe: weekly + daily tanghulu in a layered view.
  • 动量着色：山楂颜色按该价格层主动买入/卖出比例渐变
    Momentum coloring: tint berries by active buy/sell ratio at each level.

5 技术实现要点 Technical Implementation

Canvas 2D 体积正比映射 area-proportional 可扩展数据接口 extensible API 纯原生实现，无第三方金融图表库。架构分三层：① 数据层 DataManager 负责 OHLC + 价格层分布生成与 POC/VA 计算，预留 loadTicks() 接入真实逐笔数据；② 渲染层 TanghuluRenderer 用 Canvas 绘制坐标系、竹签、山楂（面积 ∝ volume，半径 = k·√volume）、POC 光晕、VA 色带、开收盘标记；③ 交互层 负责命中检测、tooltip、开关控制。山楂面积采用 √volume 映射避免极端值过大，POC 用金色描边环突出，Value Area 从 POC 向两侧贪心扩展至累计 70% 成交量。

Pure native implementation, no third-party charting library. Three-layer architecture: ① Data layer DataManager generates OHLC + price-level distributions and computes POC/VA, with loadTicks() reserved for real tick data; ② Render layer TanghuluRenderer draws axes, skewers, berries (area ∝ volume, radius = k·√volume), POC glow, VA bands and open/close ticks via Canvas; ③ Interaction layer handles hit-testing, tooltip and toggles. Berry area uses √volume mapping to tame outliers; POC is emphasized with a flat gold ring; Value Area expands greedily from POC both ways until 70% of volume is covered.
