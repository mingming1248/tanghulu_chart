Volume Profile · Per-Day Candy Skewer

糖葫芦图 Tanghulu Chart

A PRICE · VOLUME FUSION CHART

每一根"糖葫芦"代表一个交易日：串上不同大小的糖果代表不同价格层的成交量， 金圈标出 POC 最大成交价，暖色光晕圈出 Value Area 主要成交区域，木棍两端分别是最低价与最高价， 左侧空心结记开盘、右侧实心结记收盘。

Each "tanghulu" skewer represents one trading day: candies of different sizes strung along the stick encode volume at each price level. The gold ring marks the POC (highest-volume price), the warm glow band marks the Value Area (the range holding ~70% of the day's volume), the stick's two ends are Low and High, and the hollow knot (left) / solid knot (right) mark Open and Close.

数据为统计模拟生成，用于演示"价格分层 + 成交量分布"的图形编码方式； 真实场景下可将 computeVolumeProfile() 替换为基于逐笔成交 / Level2 数据的真实统计，接口保持不变。
Data is statistically simulated to demonstrate the "price-binned volume distribution" encoding; in production, swap computeVolumeProfile() for real tick / Level-2 aggregation — the downstream interface is unchanged.
