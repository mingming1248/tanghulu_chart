01 · Design Explanation

How the encoding works 编码方式说明

SKEWER · 签子

The thin vertical line spans High→Low, exactly like a candlestick's wick. Its color (amber = up day, crimson = down day) gives the direction at a glance.

竖线表示当日最高价到最低价的范围，颜色区分涨跌（金色=上涨，深红=下跌）。

BEADS · 糖珠

Each price level within the day's range is a bead split down the middle: left half = buy volume, right half = sell volume. Radius scales with √volume so area, not radius, is proportional to size.

每个价格层是一颗左右分裂的"糖珠"：左半径=主买量，右半径=主卖量，半径按成交量的平方根缩放，使面积与成交量成正比。

POC · 控制点

The bead with the highest total volume gets a gold ring. A dashed gold line connects POCs across sessions, tracing where the "center of gravity" of trading migrates over time.

总成交量最大的糖珠会被金色圆环标出；虚线金色路径连接各日POC，呈现成交重心的迁移轨迹。

VALUE AREA · 价值区

The amber ribbon marks the contiguous price band holding ~70% of the day's volume, computed by expanding outward from the POC toward whichever neighboring level has more volume. Adjacent days' ribbons tile together into a continuous "value migration" band.

琥珀色色带表示约70%成交量所在的连续价格区间（从POC向两侧成交量较大的方向扩展得到），相邻交易日的色带首尾相连，形成价值区迁移的可视化条带。

TICKS · 开收盘刻度

A short tick to the left of the skewer marks Open; a tick to the right marks Close — the same convention as a classic OHLC bar chart, preserved for continuity.

签子左侧短横线标记开盘价，右侧标记收盘价，沿用传统OHLC条形图的读图习惯。

DELTA PANEL · Delta面板

Beneath the main chart, one bar per session shows net Delta (total Buy Vol − Sell Vol) for the whole day — cyan for net buying, magenta for net selling — as a fast macro summary of the bead-level detail above.

主图下方的Delta面板以柱状图展示每日净成交差（主买-主卖），青色为净买入、洋红为净卖出，作为上方逐价位细节的宏观速览。

All shapes stay inside the standard Time (x) × Price (y) plane — no third axis, no terrain, no 3D projection. Every visual channel (position, size, color, hue split, opacity) maps to a distinct, real quantity.

所有图形都保持在标准的 时间(x) × 价格(y) 平面内，不引入第三维度、不使用地形图或三维投影。每一种视觉通道（位置、大小、颜色、色相分裂、透明度）都对应一个独立且真实的数据量。

02 · Interpretation Guide

How to read it 读图指南

  • Symmetrical, fat beads at a level mean heavy two-sided trading with no clear winner — a balanced, "accepted" price.

    某价位糖珠对称且较大：表示成交活跃但买卖势均力敌，是被市场"接受"的均衡价格。

  • Lopsided beads (one side visibly bulging) reveal which side was in control at that exact price — cyan-heavy means buyers were paying up, magenta-heavy means sellers were hitting bids aggressively.

    糖珠明显偏向一侧：偏青色说明买方在该价位主动性更强，偏洋红说明卖方更主动地砸盘。

  • A tight, smoothly migrating POC path suggests an orderly, trending market that keeps finding new acceptance; a jagged, jumping POC path suggests a choppy, indecisive market.

    POC路径平滑迁移代表趋势有序、价格不断被重新接受；路径剧烈跳动则代表市场犹豫不决、结构混乱。

  • Two sessions with near-identical OHLC can still show completely different bead patterns — one might have cyan-dominant beads near the low (buyers absorbing selling into weakness) while another has magenta-dominant beads near the high (sellers distributing into strength). The candlestick alone would never reveal this.

    两根OHLC几乎相同的K线，糖珠形态可能完全不同——一根可能在低点附近以青色为主（买方在弱势中吸筹），另一根可能在高点附近以洋红为主（卖方在强势中出货），这是传统K线完全无法体现的信息。

  • The Delta panel diverging from price direction (e.g. price closes lower but the Delta bar is cyan/positive) flags a potential absorption or exhaustion day worth a closer look at the bead detail above it.

    当Delta柱与价格方向背离时（例如收盘下跌但Delta为正/青色），提示当日可能存在吸筹或衰竭现象，值得回看上方糖珠细节。

The tradeoff is deliberate: a candlestick is faster to scan across hundreds of bars, while a Tanghulu Chart carries more truth per bar at the cost of needing more visual attention per session — closer to a compact footprint / market-profile chart than a plain OHLC bar.

这种取舍是有意为之：K线便于快速扫视上百根柱子，而糖葫芦图在单根柱子上承载更多真实信息，代价是每个交易日需要更多注意力——其定位更接近"足迹图/市场轮廓图"，而非单纯的OHLC柱状图。

04 · Future Extensions

Where this could go next 后续扩展方向

  • Replace the simulated multi-modal Gaussian profile with real tick-by-tick or Level 2 order-flow data — the data model already separates "OHLC" from "per-price buy/sell volume" so this is a drop-in swap.

    用真实逐笔成交或Level 2订单流数据替换当前模拟的多峰高斯分布——数据模型已将OHLC与逐价位买卖量拆分，可直接替换数据源。

  • Cumulative Volume Delta (CVD) overlay across sessions, plotted as a secondary line to spot divergence with price at a glance.

    叠加累计成交量差（CVD）曲线，直观展示与价格走势的背离。

  • Multi-timeframe composite: merge intraday sessions into a single weekly/monthly Tanghulu bead using the same aggregation logic as the value-area algorithm.

    多周期合成：将日内多个交易时段合并为单个周/月糖葫芦糖珠，复用价值区算法进行聚合。

  • Absorption/exhaustion pattern detector: automatically flag sessions where Delta direction diverges from price direction (as described in the interpretation guide) with a small marker above the skewer.

    吸筹/衰竭形态自动识别：当Delta方向与价格方向背离时，在签子上方自动标记提示。

  • Live streaming mode via WebSocket, redrawing only the current (rightmost) session's beads as new prints arrive, instead of full regeneration.

    通过WebSocket接入实时数据，仅重绘最右侧当前交易日的糖珠，而非整体重新生成。

  • Alternate palette toggle for color-vision deficiency (e.g. blue/orange instead of cyan/magenta), plus a "print/export as PNG" button for sharing a specific session range.

    提供色觉障碍友好的配色切换（如蓝/橙替代青/洋红），并增加导出PNG功能便于分享特定区间。

  • Side-by-side symbol comparison: two Tanghulu charts sharing a synchronized time axis and crosshair, for relative-strength analysis.

    多标的并排对比：两个糖葫芦图共享同步的时间轴与十字光标，用于相对强弱分析。
