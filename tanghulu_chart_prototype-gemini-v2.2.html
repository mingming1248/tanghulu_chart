1 图表设计理念 Design Concept

核心隐喻：一根竹签串着若干糖果——这正是中国街头小吃"糖葫芦"的形态。 Core metaphor: a bamboo skewer threading several candies — exactly the shape of the Chinese street snack "tanghulu".

  • 竹签 (Skewer)：代表当日 Low → High 的价格区间。
    Represents the day's Low → High price range.
  • 糖果 (Candies)：每个价格层的成交量。糖果的宽度与该层成交量成正比，越宽代表该价格成交越密集。
    Volume at each price level. Width is proportional to volume — wider means heavier trading.
  • 买卖失衡 (Delta Split)：(V2创新) 左右不对称设计。左侧(青色)代表主买成交量(Ask Vol)，右侧(红色)代表主卖成交量(Bid Vol)。哪边更鼓，说明该价位哪方力量更激进。
    (V2 Innovation) Asymmetrical design. Left (Cyan) = Ask Vol (Buy), Right (Red) = Bid Vol (Sell). A bulge on one side indicates which side was more aggressive at that price.

2 如何解读糖胡芦图 How to Read

  • 看糖果分布：糖果集中在哪段价格，就是当日的成交重心。越聚拢说明市场共识越强。
    Berry distribution: where candies cluster is the day's trading center of gravity.
  • 看最宽处 (POC)：金色横线标记的位置，是当日成交量最大的价格 (Point of Control)。
    Widest point (POC): the gold line marks the price with the most volume.
  • 看高亮区 (Value Area)：颜色更实、覆盖约 70% 成交量的价格区间，是当日价值区域。
    Highlighted zone (Value Area): the heavy-fill area covering ~70% of volume.
  • 看左右不对称：如果某价位左侧青色明显宽于右侧红色，说明买方在此处极其激进（主动吃筹码）。
    Asymmetry: if left (cyan) is much wider than right (red) at a price, buyers were highly aggressive there.
  • 看跨日迁移：横向对比多日的 POC 连线，可清晰看到市场公允价值的抬升或下降趋势。
    Cross-day migration: tracking the POC across days clearly shows the trend of fair value.

3 相比传统K线的优势 Advantages over Candlesticks

  • 信息密度极高：传统K线只有 4 个价格点；糖胡芦图同时展示了完整的成交量分布曲线、POC、VA和多空失衡。
    Higher density: candlesticks have 4 points; tanghulu adds the full volume profile, POC, VA, and buy/sell imbalance.
  • 揭示真实筹码结构：两根外表相同的K线，内部可能完全不同。糖胡芦图能一眼看穿市场究竟是在高位派发还是低位吸筹。
    Reveals cost structure: two identical candles can hide completely different internal structures. Tanghulu exposes accumulation vs distribution.
  • 识别假突破：价格虽然突破，但 POC 和 VA 如果没有跟随上移，往往意味着量价背离，突破有效性存疑。
    Spot fake breakouts: if price breaks but POC/VA don't follow, it suggests volume/price divergence.
