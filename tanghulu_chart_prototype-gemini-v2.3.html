1 图表设计理念 Design Concept

核心隐喻：一根竹签串着若干糖果——这正是中国街头小吃"糖葫芦"的形态。 Core metaphor: a bamboo skewer threading several candies — exactly the shape of the Chinese street snack "tanghulu".

  • 竹签 (Skewer)：代表当日 Low → High 的价格区间。
    Represents the day's Low → High price range.
  • 糖果 (Candies)：每个价格层的成交量。糖果的宽度与该层成交量成正比，越宽代表该价格成交越密集。
    Volume at each price level. Width is proportional to volume — wider means heavier trading.
  • 买卖失衡 (Delta Split)：左右不对称设计。左侧(青色)代表主买成交量(Ask Vol)，右侧(红色)代表主卖成交量(Bid Vol)。哪边更鼓，说明该价位哪方力量更激进。
    Asymmetrical design. Left (Cyan) = Ask Vol (Buy), Right (Red) = Bid Vol (Sell). A bulge on one side indicates which side was more aggressive at that price.

2 如何解读糖胡芦图 How to Read

  • 看糖果分布：糖果集中在哪段价格，就是当日的成交重心。越聚拢说明市场共识越强。
    Berry distribution: where candies cluster is the day's trading center of gravity.
  • 看最宽处 (POC)：金色横线标记的位置，是当日成交量最大的价格 (Point of Control)。
    Widest point (POC): the gold line marks the price with the most volume.
  • 看高亮区 (Value Area)：颜色更实、覆盖约 70% 成交量的价格区间，是当日价值区域。
    Highlighted zone (Value Area): the heavy-fill area covering ~70% of volume.
  • 看左右不对称：如果某价位左侧青色明显宽于右侧红色，说明买方在此处极其激进（主动吃筹码）。
    Asymmetry: if left (cyan) is much wider than right (red) at a price, buyers were highly aggressive there.

3 极致优化的开收盘 Optimized Open/Close

为解决传统糖胡芦图难以一眼辨识开收盘的问题，本版本采用了极致的符号化设计：
To solve the issue of identifying open/close prices in previous versions, we adopted an extreme symbolic design:

  • 左侧空心圆结 (Left Hollow Knot)：代表开盘价 (Open)。
    Represents the Open price.
  • 右侧实心圆结 (Right Solid Knot)：代表收盘价 (Close)。
    Represents the Close price.
  • 虚线收盘趋势线 (Close Trend Line)：将所有交易日的收盘价串联，瞬间把握市场整体趋势。
    Connects all close prices to instantly grasp the overall market trend without relying on colors.

4 智能分布模式 Smart Distribution Modes

提供四种微观筹码模拟生成模式，可在顶部工具栏切换：
Offers four simulated micro-volume generation modes, switchable via the top toolbar:

  • 智能混合 (Smart Mix)：根据日内涨跌幅自动选择分布类型。
    Automatically selects distribution type based on intraday volatility.
  • 正态分布 (Normal)：经典钟形曲线，筹码集中在均价。
    Classic bell curve, volume concentrated at the average price.
  • 双峰分布 (Bimodal)：开盘和收盘处各有一个筹码峰，说明日内两端争夺激烈。
    Volume peaks at both open and close, indicating fierce battles at the extremes.
  • 偏态分布 (Skewed)：筹码极度偏向收盘价，暗示单边趋势强劲。
    Volume heavily skewed towards the close, implying a strong one-sided trend.
